About Me

Hi, I’m Thaqif Dzakirin, a Diploma in Computer Science student at Sultan Idris Education University (UPSI).

I’ve always been someone who thinks deeply before acting, I take time to understand things fully before making a move. This reflective nature often pushes me to ask “why” and “how,” whether I’m working on a project, playing games, or learning new tech.

In group work, I usually take the lead, not because I have to, but because I care about making sure everyone contributes and grows together. I enjoy solving problems with a team and turning abstract ideas into something real and meaningful.

Outside the classroom, I’m a gamer and a reader. I enjoy stories that make me think, whether in books or in the games I play.

One fun fact: I’ve been told I look angry when I smile in pictures, but if you really know me, you’ll see I smile often. Just… maybe not always in photos.
